Macana Corp - YARA-9 Exploration Network
Hackathon Pitch Script (2-3 minutes)

Hello everyone.
We are Macana Corp, and we are building the YARA-9 Exploration Network for EVE Frontier.

Macana's origin comes from DrZeus and the YARA Team, whose research around YARA-9 pushed both industrial breakthroughs and human limits.
After the convergence events of Frontier, their mission continued in this universe: find YARA-9 again, secure its strategic value, and build the infrastructure that could one day make a return path to their origin reality possible.

Our core idea is simple:
turn exploration data into a real utility economy.

In many systems, exploration rewards are isolated events.
You scan, you loot, and the value cycle ends quickly.

We designed a different model.

Players enter a corporate exploration network with a clear progression loop:
Acquire a satellite, scan a rocky target, retrieve mineral intelligence as a data item, redeem that data at the Macana Commerce Center, receive standing and MTC rewards, and progress into advanced infrastructure.

This makes exploration strategically meaningful, not just random reward farming.

Our system has four layers:

1) Player interaction layer:
fast onboarding and readable progression.

2) Satellite scan logic layer:
tiered satellites, better precision with trade-offs, and categorized outputs from common mineral data to rare YARA-9 signature fragments.

Tier 1 satellites are fabricated by Macana with in-game resources and distributed under standing-based daily quotas.
Advanced satellites require consumable licenses crafted in MCC from base resources.

3) Macana Commerce Center layer:
the operational hub for distribution, redemption, industrial services, and access control.

4) Economic and validation layer:
before rewards are issued, data is validated with anti-abuse checks, including duplicate-window penalties.
Then MTC is granted according to rarity, quality, demand, and balance policy.

Why does this matter?

Because MTC is not an empty token.
Its value comes from what players can actually do with it:
unlock services, enter restricted routes, improve exploration capacity, and access corporate infrastructure.

And MTC emission is budget-based, backed by Macana's economic performance in LUX.
So rewards are governed, not infinite.

For this hackathon stage, we are delivering a web-based interactive prototype that demonstrates the complete loop:
Acquire -> Scan -> Redeem -> Spend.

This gives players and partners a playable experience now, and a credible path to future smart contract validation on Sui.

Our long-term vision is to build a scalable, trust-aware exploration economy where knowledge of resources becomes the most valuable resource itself.

Macana Corp does not only sell materials.
Macana sells intelligence about where value exists.

Thank you.
